Privacy & Safety

Built to handle what people don't say out loud.

Encrypted, always

Conversations are encrypted in transit and handled in line with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act. Nothing is sold, and nothing leaves the conversation it was shared in.

A human, when it matters

If a conversation ever shows signs of crisis, the system pauses its usual flow and routes the person toward real clinical support — not an automated remedy, and never a sales prompt.

Known limits, on purpose

Severe psychiatric presentations, trauma, fertility, and anyone under 18 are explicitly outside the automated system's scope. The platform is strongest when it knows where it ends.

Astro Vaidhya is an AI-powered wellness information tool and is not a substitute for professional medical, psychiatric or psychological care. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for diagnosis and treatment. If you're in crisis, please reach out to a licensed professional or your local emergency services right away.
