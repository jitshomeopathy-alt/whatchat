Who we are

Astro Vaidhya is an AI-guided homeopathic wellness service that lives inside WhatsApp. We built it to make thoughtful, judgement-free case-taking available to anyone with a phone — no apps to download, no accounts to create, no waiting rooms.

What we do

Through a single WhatsApp conversation, Astro Vaidhya guides you from the sensation that's actually present toward a constitutional reading suited to what you've described. It works across three wellness verticals, each with its own adaptive line of questions.

  • Mental wellness — grief, anxiety, burnout, loneliness and the quieter, newer states.
  • Addiction recovery — substance patterns and behavioural ones alike, built around the person, not the habit.
  • Intimate wellness — concerns rarely spoken aloud, handled with the same clinical care as everything else (18+).

Clinically directed, not just AI-assisted

Astro Vaidhya is built and clinically directed by Dr. Jitendra Pal (BHMS, MD Homeopathy). Every branch — from the first question to the final remedy — has been written and audited against classical repertories and current Indian and global research, not generated from a generic model and left unchecked.

Known limits, on purpose

When a conversation moves into territory that needs a human — a sign of crisis, a case too complex for an automated remedy, a question of fertility or trauma, or anyone under 18 — it's routed to a licensed physician directly, with the full context already in hand. The platform is strongest when it knows where it ends.

Important note

Astro Vaidhya is an AI-powered wellness information tool and is not a substitute for professional medical, psychiatric or psychological care. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for diagnosis and treatment.
